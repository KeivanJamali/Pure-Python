# Meet ChatGPT: Your New Digital Assistant

## Slide 1: What is ChatGPT?
* **Concept:** Imagine having a very smart, well-read assistant who is awake 24/7 and ready to help you with almost anything. It is a computer program that you can talk to just like a human.
* **Real World Example:** Instead of searching Google and reading 10 different websites to find an answer, you just ask ChatGPT a question, and it writes the exact answer for you immediately.
* **Prompt to try:** *"I am planning a 3-day trip to Vienna. I love classical music and visiting old cafes. Can you write a day-by-day itinerary for me?"*

## Slide 2: Keeping Things Fresh (Why New Chats Matter)
* **Concept:** Just like humans, if you talk about too many different things in one long conversation, ChatGPT can get "exhausted" or confused. It might mix up your previous topics.
* **The Rule:** Always start a **New Chat** for a new topic. If you were talking about a recipe, and now want to talk about a rock band, hit the "New Chat" button!
* **Real World Example:** If you have a chat where you are asking for help writing a formal email to your boss, don't suddenly ask it for a pizza recipe in that same chat. Start a new one!

## Slide 3: Your Personal Translator
* **Concept:** You can turn ChatGPT into a dedicated, lightning-fast translator.
* **How to do it:** Start a new chat and give it one single instruction at the beginning.
* **Prompt to copy and paste:** *"From now on, whatever I type in Ukrainian, translate it to English. And whatever I type in English, translate it to Ukrainian. Do not add any other text, just give me the translation."*
* **The Magic:** You never have to ask it to translate again in that specific chat. Just open that chat, type your text, and it will automatically translate it every time!

## Slide 4: Searching the Web & Finding Links
* **Concept:** ChatGPT isn't just a brain; it can also search the live internet for you.
* **Real World Example:** You want to buy tickets for a concert, but you don't know where to look or what the official website is.
* **Prompt to try:** *"Find me the official website to buy tickets for the upcoming Coldplay concert in Warsaw, and give me the direct link."*

## Slide 5: Seeing the World Through Pictures
* **Concept:** ChatGPT has "eyes"! You can upload photos from your phone and ask questions about them.
* **Real World Example:** You are walking in the park and see a beautiful, strange flower, or you are in a store and see a gadget you don't understand.
* **How to do it:** Snap a picture, upload it to ChatGPT, and ask:
* **Prompt to try:** *"What kind of flower is this in the picture? How often does it need to be watered if I keep it in my apartment?"*

## Slide 6: A Listening Ear (Psychological Support)
* **Concept:** Sometimes we all feel a little sad, stressed, or just need someone to talk to. 
* **Real World Example:** You had a very stressful day at work and you just want to vent, or you are feeling anxious about an upcoming event.
* **Prompt to try:** *"I had a really terrible day today and I feel very stressed. Can you just listen to me and offer some comforting words?"*

## Slide 7: Everyday Helper (Cooking, Health, and a Warning!)
* **Concept:** It’s a fantastic assistant for daily life. You can tell it what ingredients are in your fridge, and it will give you a step-by-step recipe.
* **Real World Example:** You open your fridge and only see chicken, rice, and some carrots.
* **Prompt to try:** *"I only have chicken breast, white rice, and carrots in my kitchen. What is a simple and tasty dinner I can make with just these ingredients?"*
* **The Warning:** While it can give general health or wellness advice, **never trust AI blindly with serious matters**. It is not a real doctor. Always verify important health, medical, or safety information with a human professional.

## Slide 8: The Ultimate Teacher (Math, Science & Tech)
* **Concept:** ChatGPT is brilliant at explaining complicated things in a very simple way.
* **Real World Example:** You hear people talking about "Blockchain" or "Quantum Physics" on the news, and you have no idea what it means.
* **Prompt to try:** *"Explain to me what a Black Hole is, but explain it using simple words like I am a 10-year-old child."*

## Slide 9: Let's Try It!
* **Concept:** The best way to learn is by doing. Let's open the app and try one of the prompts right now!
