## **SLIDE 10: Virtual Traffic Lights (VTL)** (1 minute)

**Title:** Virtual Traffic Lights: The Future of Intersections

**How VTL Works:**

1. 5G + Road Infrastructure (cameras, sensors) detect vehicles
2. Virtual signals projected or sent to vehicle OBUs (On-Board Units)
3. Real-time collision warnings to drivers
4. Automatic braking system activation if needed

**Key Benefits:**

- ‚úÖ Perfect for intersections without physical traffic lights
- ‚úÖ Real-time stop warnings for approaching vehicles
- ‚úÖ Pedestrian crossing protection
- ‚úÖ Emergency vehicle prioritization
- ‚úÖ Reduced congestion and idling time

**Critical Requirement:** 5G technology (high-speed, low-latency)
