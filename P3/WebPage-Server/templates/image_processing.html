Image Processing

This file doesn't need installation. You move it to your local PC and run it whenever you need it.

Purpose:

Change the sorting of your images into folders suitable for apps. This app does not alter your images at all. It only makes a copy of your images in the result folder. Ensure you have enough space, as the output images will be created in the specified output folder.

Parameters:

  • Main Folder: The folder where images are located. Images can be in subfolders, but they should be in a write sequence according to the name of the folder and filename.
  • Output Folder: Choose a folder where the output will be stored.
  • Minutes Limit: The limit you think is suitable for your images. For example, if your images are taken at the rate of 10 seconds or 1 minute, and you need to sort images for 10 minutes, you can set this limit to 5 minutes. 5 is a good number in most cases.
